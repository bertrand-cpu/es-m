Admissions

Join Ecole Secondaire de Murama and become part of a community dedicated to academic excellence and character development.

Welcome to Excellence Academy

We warmly welcome students who are eager to learn, grow, and succeed in a supportive and inspiring educational environment.

Our admission process is simple, transparent, and designed to help every learner begin their academic journey successfully.

Admission Process

1. Application

Complete the admission application form.

2. Submit Documents

Provide previous report cards and birth certificate.

3. Assessment

Applicants may complete an entrance assessment.

4. Interview

Parents and students meet with school administration.

5. Acceptance

Receive an admission offer and enrollment details.

6. Enrollment

Complete registration and begin your studies.

Birth Certificate

Submit a copy of the student's birth certificate.

Academic Records

Previous school report cards or transcripts.

Passport Photos

Two recent passport-size photographs.
